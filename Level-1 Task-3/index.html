About Us

Welcome to Antonio Snacks, where flavor meets tradition! Established in 1992 by the visionary entrepreneur Anthony Thompson , our snacks embody a legacy of excellence. Crafted with the finest ingredients and a dash of creativity, each bite is a journey into culinary delight. Alongside Anthony, our co-owner Christina Evans adds her expertise, ensuring that every snack is a masterpiece. From savory burgers to decadent cakes, our menu offers a tantalizing array of options to satisfy every craving. At Antonio Snacks, quality and passion are our guiding principles, driving us to deliver an unforgettable dining experience. Join us on this flavorful adventure and discover why Antonio Snacks is more than just a snack company—it's a celebration of taste and tradition. So come on in, explore our menu, and savor the magic of Antonio Snacks.
